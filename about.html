The Story

Ssekito Mark AbdulRahim

At just 18 years old, Smark Tat is bringing a fresh wave of energy to the Ugandan music scene. Born Ssekito Mark AbdulRahim, he has always had rhythm and lyrics running through his veins.

Bursting onto the scene with his debut single "Ndongo Yoka", Smark Tat proves that age is just a number when it comes to raw talent and musical vision. This is just the beginning of a legendary portfolio.
