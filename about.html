The Story

Ssekito Mark AbdulRahim, professionally known as Smark Tat, is an 18-year-old Ugandan musician carving his path in the industry.

With the release of his debut single "Ndongo Yoka," Smark Tat has shown that age is no barrier to talent. Combining rhythmic innovation with deep-rooted Ugandan vibes, he is the new face of the youth music movement.
