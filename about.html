The Artist

Ssekito Mark AbdulRahim, known professionally as Smark Tat, is a powerhouse of talent emerging from the heart of Uganda.

At just 18 years old, Smark Tat represents the voice of the new generation. His sound is a fusion of cultural roots and modern rhythmic energy, perfectly captured in his debut single, "Ndongo Yoka".

With a vision to redefine the Ugandan music landscape, he balances his youth with a professional drive that is rare to find. This portfolio is a testament to his journey—one that is only just beginning.
